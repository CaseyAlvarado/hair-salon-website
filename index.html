Meet Maria

Hair stylist for 25 years

: rosemartinez20ma@gmail.com



Twenty five years ago I made the decision to go to cosmetology school in downtown Houston, Texas. Since then I have worked at several places like Fantastic Sam, Sports Clip, and more. Nine years ago, I started working at Hair Desire Salon, a salon in the Houston Heights area. I really like meeting people of the Houston Heights community. I want to give everyone a hair cut or hair style that will make them feel great about themselves!
